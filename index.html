How to play Tic Tac Toe

To play Tic Tac Toe, players take turns placing their symbol (either an "X" or an "O") in an empty square. The first player to get three of their symbols in a row, column, or diagonal wins the game. If all squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a draw.
